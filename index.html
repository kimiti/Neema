Why Choose Neema Hospital?

Neema Hospital is located at Kahawa Sukari along the Thika Super Highway (Exit No. 10). This is a level 4 mission hospital designed to provide quality and affordable healthcare to all. It operates 24 hours daily under the management of a highly qualified team led by a medical officer in-change. Other medical staff include Nurses, Lab & Pharmaceutical Technologists.

Inpatient & Outpatient

We offer quality service to both our Inpatient and Outpatient clients, dedicated Male, female and Children wards. and open 24 hours. visit us for more details and enquiries.

Theater

Our theater is well equiped to handle minor and Major surgical operations. The theater also encompass a Post-operative ward .

Maternity

We offer quality Pre natal, Delivery and Post natal services at affordable rates. Our Delivery room is also well equipped .
